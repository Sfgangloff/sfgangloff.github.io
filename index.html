Research interests:

I am interested in symbolic dynamical systems called subshifts of finite type (SFT). These systems are defined by the action of the shift on a set of layouts of symbols in a finite set, defined by local rules on these symbols. As topological dynamical systems, they are studied through concepts of topological dynamics. Moreover, their properties are related computability theory. I am interested in how dynamical constraints affect these properties.
