<p align="center" id="head_paragraph_workgroup" class="section_head"> 			
			Work group: exact computation of entropy for multidimensional SFT 
		</p>
		
		<br>
		

<table class="flex_table">
	<tr>
		<td style="width:20%">
    			<img src="files_svg/Standalone(7).svg" id="image_workgroup" style="width:80%;display:block;
    			margin-right:auto;"/>
		</td> 
	
		<td class="flex_table_paragraph">
		
        
			During the academic year 2018-2019 I organised a work group 
			aimed at studying exact computation methods 
			for entropy of multidimensional subshifts of finite type developped 
			in statistical physics and in particular: to provide 
			rigorous tools for proving the predictions of physicists; 
			and to understand the conditions under which the entropy 
			of bidimensional SFT can be algorithmically computable. 
			This direction of research is still part of my project and 
			I leave the titles of talks presented during this work group for further reference. 
        	</td> 
        </tr>
</table>
